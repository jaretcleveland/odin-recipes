New York Style Pizza Recipe

Description

This recipe was created to mimic the quality of pizza found in New York City. The recipe relies on only a few quality ingredients and a bit of patience. Making the dough from scratch takes time, we want at least 24 but idealy 48 to ferment our dough before baking. It is required that you have a baking steel to follow this recipe.

Ingredients:

(Makes two 329g dough balls, stretch to ~14")

  • 387g Flour
  • 9.7g Salt
  • 1.0g Instant yeast
  • 5.4g Sugar
  • 8g Olive oil
  • 247g Water
  • 32oz Can of quality whole peepled tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Whole milk low moisture mozzarella

Steps for making pizza dough:

  1. Start by mixing you dry ingredients in a bowl (flour, salt, sugar, and yeast).
  2. Add the room temperature water and olive oil and mix with a wooden spoon until roughly combined.
  3. Remove everything from the bowl onto a large work surface and kneed the dough for 7-8 minutes.
  4. Ball up the dough and let rest covered for 20 minutes.
  5. After 20 minutes, strech and fold the dough from each side. Reapeat this step until the dough passes the window pane test.
  6. Portion the dough into prefered sizes, tightly ball each portion up and place into a lighlty oil container.
  7. Let ferment in refrigerator for 48-72 hours.

Steps making pizza sauce:

  1. Use an immersion blender to blend tomatoes.
  2. Add one table spoon of salt (for a 32 oz can of tomatoes)
  3. Add a dash of dried oregeno.
  4. Optionally add some cut up basil. You can also add this after baking.

Steps for baking the pizza:

  1. 3-4 hours before baking, remove pizza dough(s) from the refrigerator and let them rest at room temp.
  2. Preheat you over to its highest temp on convection setting, at least 1 hour prior to baking. This allows you baking steel to heat up.
  3. "Opening up" your pizza dough can be hard to explain. Follow this video to do so.
  4. Spoon on some sauce and evenly distribute it over the dough without adding too much. Remember, you can always add more.
  5. Freshly grade you mozzarella cheese onto the pie. Again, not too much. New York Style pizza should be relatively thin.
  6. Drizzle on some olive oil and launch the pizza into the oven.
  7. After about 3 minutes, turn the pizza 50 degrees (to bake evenly). Bake for an aditional 4-5 minutes.
  8. Enjoy!
